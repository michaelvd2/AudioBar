AudioBar Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

AudioBar is a local macOS menu bar utility for source volume control and system audio EQ. AudioBar does not sell, rent, or share personal data.

Audio

AudioBar requests system audio capture permission so it can process audio locally for the EQ route. AudioBar does not record audio, save audio, upload audio, or transmit audio to any server.

Automation

When you use Music, Spotify, or Safari controls, AudioBar may ask macOS for permission to send Apple Events to that app. Those events are used only to perform the control you selected, such as playback or volume changes.

Data Collection

AudioBar stores local preferences on your Mac, such as EQ settings, hidden sources, and saved source volumes. AudioBar does not collect analytics, identifiers, diagnostics, contact information, location, browsing history, or user content.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed on the AudioBar Support page.
